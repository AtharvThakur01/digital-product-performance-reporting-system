# QA Playbook

## Data QA

- Confirm dashboard data sources refresh before stakeholder reporting.
- Check product names, owner fields, and metric definitions against the metric dictionary.
- Review missing values, duplicated product rows, and unexpected metric spikes.
- Compare incident counts with the production incident register.

## Product QA

- Review open defects by severity before release decisions.
- Confirm high-risk products have an accountable owner and target date.
- Monitor SLA attainment after major launches or integrations.
- Document known limitations in weekly reporting notes.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Dashboard loads with product and period filters.
- Each product has adoption, task success, defects, SLA, health, and owner data.
- Risk queue prioritizes products with defects, incidents, or SLA pressure.
- Stakeholder reporting framework is documented in the repository.
