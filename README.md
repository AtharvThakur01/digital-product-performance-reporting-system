# Digital Product Performance & Reporting System

A portfolio-ready reporting system for digital products and services. It combines dashboard reporting, operational insights, quality assurance, and stakeholder-ready product performance summaries.

## Highlights

- Interactive dashboard for product adoption, task success, SLA, defects, incidents, and health.
- Product and reporting period filters.
- QA playbook for validating product and data quality.
- Reporting framework with a metric dictionary and stakeholder operating rhythm.
- Sample product performance dataset.

## Run Locally

Open `index.html` in a browser. No build step is required.

## Business Scenario

A product operations team needs a weekly system that turns product analytics, QA defects, incidents, and service reliability signals into practical reporting. This project demonstrates how to structure performance dashboards and stakeholder insights for digital product teams.
